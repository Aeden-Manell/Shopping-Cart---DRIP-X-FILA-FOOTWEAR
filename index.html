About - FILA X DRIP Collaboration

FILA Partners With Local Footwear Brand Drip For An Iconic Limited Edition Collection

The collaboration between FILA and Drip is an exciting partnership that brings together two renowned brands in the footwear industry.

Proudly South African Brand Drip together with International Italian Brand FILA, both largely known for being game changers in sneaker and sports apparel – are excited to announce a joint new range of streetwear inspired by sport and growing urban styles.

Celebrating their uniquely different heritage that birthed leaders in trendsetting, FILA and Drip have collaborated to create 3 fashion drops that were produced, designed and inspired by Drips’ signature bold designs, together with FILAs’ dedication to basketball and sport.
